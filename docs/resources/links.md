## 📖 Recommended Books

### Translations of Classic Works

**"The Shahnameh" translated by Dick Davis**

- Complete English translation of Firdawsi's epic
- Accessible and poetic English rendering

**"Rumi: The Book of Love" translated by Nader Khalili**

- Selection of Rumi's poetry in beautiful translation
- Includes Persian text and English rendering

**"The Conference of the Birds" by Attar, translated by Afkham Darbandi**

- Sufi mystical epic poem
- Profound spiritual insights

**"The Rubaiyat of Omar Khayyam" translated by Robert Graves**

- Philosophical quatrains
- Multiple modern translation versions available

**"The Bustan and Golestan of Saadi" translated by John H. Little**

- Wisdom poetry by Saadi
- Practical philosophical insights
