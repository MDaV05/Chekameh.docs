# AI Chatbot

## 💬 Your Persian Poetry Companion

Our intelligent chatbot is here to help you explore and learn about Persian poetry in an interactive, conversational way.

## 🤖 What Can the Chatbot Do?

### Answer Questions

Ask about:

- Specific poets and their biographies
- Historical periods and their characteristics
- Famous poems and their meanings
- Geographic centers of literary activity
- Cultural and historical context

### Provide Recommendations

The chatbot can suggest:

- Poets to explore based on your interests
- Famous works from specific eras
- Cities with rich poetic heritage
- Related topics for further study

### Guide Your Exploration

Get help with:

- Navigating the interactive map
- Understanding historical timelines
- Finding connections between poets
- Discovering lesser-known literary figures

## 🎯 How to Use the Chatbot

1. **Start a Conversation** - Click the chatbot icon on the website
2. **Ask Naturally** - Type your questions in plain language
3. **Follow Up** - Ask follow-up questions for deeper understanding
4. **Explore Links** - Click on suggested links to dive deeper

## 💡 Example Questions

Try asking:

- "Who was Hafez and what is he known for?"
- "What poets lived during the Safavid era?"
- "Which city was the center of poetry in the 14th century?"
- "Tell me about the Shahnameh"
- "What's the difference between ghazal and qasida?"

## 🔧 Technology Behind the Chatbot

Our chatbot is built using modern AI technologies to provide accurate and helpful responses about Persian poetry and culture.
